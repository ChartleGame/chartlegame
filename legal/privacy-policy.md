# Chartle — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Chartle ("the Platform," "the Service," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your information when you use our platform. By using Chartle, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address
- Username
- Password (stored as a cryptographic hash — we never store your plain-text password)

### 1.2 Gameplay Data
When you use the Platform, we collect:

- Daily chart trade data (direction, stop loss, take profit, score, result)
- Practice session usage counts
- Consensus votes
- Trade journal entries and notes you create

### 1.3 Technical Data
When you access the Platform, we may automatically collect:

- Browser type and version
- Device type
- IP address
- Cookies (see Section 5)

### 1.4 Payment Data
If you subscribe to a paid plan in the future, payment information will be collected and processed directly by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. We may store a Stripe customer ID and subscription ID to manage your account.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the Platform and its features
- Authenticate your identity and secure your account
- Track your daily chart attempts and practice usage
- Display community consensus data (aggregated and anonymous)
- Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets)
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
- Delete unverified accounts after 24 hours

We do not use your information to provide financial advice or trading recommendations. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 3. Data Storage and Infrastructure

### 3.1 Where Your Data Is Stored
Your data is stored on servers hosted by **Railway** (railway.app), a third-party cloud infrastructure provider.

### 3.2 Data Retention
We retain your personal data for the maximum period permitted by applicable law, or until you delete your account, whichever comes first. Unverified accounts are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

### 3.3 Account Deletion
When you delete your account (or when we terminate it), we delete your personal data from our database, including your account information, journal entries, and associated records. Some anonymous or aggregated data (such as consensus votes that are not tied to your identity) may be retained.

**Important: While we delete your data from our own database upon account deletion, your data is hosted on Railway's infrastructure. We cannot guarantee that Railway will immediately or permanently erase all copies of your data from their systems, including backups, logs, or redundant storage. Railway's handling of data is governed by their own privacy policy and data retention practices, which are outside of our control.** We encourage you to review Railway's privacy policy at [https://railway.app/legal/privacy](https://railway.app/legal/privacy) for more information.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the Platform:

- **Railway** (railway.app) — Server hosting and database infrastructure
- **Stripe** (stripe.com) — Payment processing (when applicable)
- **Resend** (resend.com) — Transactional email delivery (verification and password reset emails)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We are not responsible for the data practices of these third-party providers. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

## 5. Cookies

The Platform uses a limited number of cookies:

- **Authentication token** — Stored in your browser's localStorage to keep you signed in. This is cleared when you sign out or delete your account.
- **Anonymous player ID** — If you use the Platform without an account, a cookie (`chartle_anon`) is set to identify your session for gameplay purposes (such as tracking your daily chart attempt). This cookie expires after one year. It contains a randomly generated ID and does not track you across other websites.

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking cookies.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Aggregated data** — We may display aggregated, anonymous gameplay data (such as community consensus statistics) that cannot be used to identify you personally.
- **Service providers** — We share data with the third-party services listed in Section 4 solely to operate the Platform (for example, your email address is shared with Resend to deliver verification emails).
- **Legal requirements** — We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

## 7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
- **Delete** your account and personal data (available through the account settings page).
- **Object** to or restrict certain processing of your data.
- **Data portability** — request a copy of your data in a structured format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at **chartle@chartlegame.com**. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at **chartle@chartlegame.com**.

The Platform is intended for use by individuals located in Canada and other jurisdictions outside the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland. **The Platform is not designed to comply with the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the UK Data Protection Act, and is not intended for, directed at, or offered to individuals subject to those regulations.**

By using the Platform, you represent and warrant that you are not located in, and are not a resident of, any jurisdiction where the GDPR or equivalent legislation applies. If you are located in the EEA, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you should not use the Platform.

The Platform is hosted in data centers managed by Railway, which may be located in various regions. By using the Platform, you consent to the transfer of your data to the jurisdiction where our servers are located, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.
